Rules and Basics –

This page will explain the rules on how to play Pokémon TCG.

Summary:

ObjectiveKnock out your opponent’s Pokémon and take all your Prize cards to win.SetupEach player needs a 60-card deck (no more, no less).Shuffle your deck and draw 7 cards for your starting hand.Place one Basic Pokémon face down as your Active Pokémon (if you have one).Place up to 5 Basic Pokémon on your Bench.Set aside 6 Prize cards face down (these are claimed when you knock out an opponent’s Pokémon).Flip a coin to decide who goes first.Turn StructureDraw a card (except on the first turn for the player who goes first).Play Basic Pokémon to your Bench (if available).Attach one Energy card per turn to a Pokémon.Evolve Pokémon if you have the next stage in your hand (you can’t evolve on the first turn or the turn a Pokémon is played).Play Trainer cards (Item cards anytime, Supporter & Stadium cards once per turn).Use Abilities (if applicable).Attack with your Active Pokémon (if it has enough Energy).Calculate damage based on the attack description.Apply Weakness & Resistance.Knock out an opponent’s Pokémon if damage equals or exceeds their HP (take a Prize card if you do).End your turn.Winning the GameTake all 6 Prize cards by knocking out Pokémon.If your opponent has no Pokémon left in play.If your opponent can’t draw a card at the start of their turn.
