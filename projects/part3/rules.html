Rules and Basics –

This page will explain the rules on how to play Pokémon TCG.

Summary:

Objective

Knock out your opponent’s Pokémon and take all your Prize cards to win.

Setup

Each player needs a 60-card deck (no more, no less).

Shuffle your deck and draw 7 cards for your starting hand.

Place one Basic Pokémon face down as your Active Pokémon (if you have one).

Place up to 5 Basic Pokémon on your Bench.

Set aside 6 Prize cards face down (these are claimed when you knock out an opponent’s Pokémon).

Flip a coin to decide who goes first.>
