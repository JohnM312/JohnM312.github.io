Rules and Basics

This page will explain the rules on how to play Pokémon TCG.

Summary:

  • Knock out your opponent’s Pokémon and collect Prize cards.
  • Each player has a 60-card deck.
  • Basic gameplay steps include drawing, attaching energy, and attacking.
  • Win by collecting all Prize cards or if the opponent cannot draw a card.
