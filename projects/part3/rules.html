Rules and Basics –

This page will explain the rules on how to play Pokémon TCG.

Summary:

Objective

Knock out your opponent’s Pokémon and take all your Prize cards to win.

Setup

Each player needs a 60-card deck, shuffles, and draws 7 cards. Place one Basic Pokémon as the Active Pokémon (if available) and up to 5 on the Bench. Set aside 6 Prize cards. Flip a coin to decide who goes first..
