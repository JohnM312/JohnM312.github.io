Pokémon Cards

Trainer Cards

Help players in different ways, such as drawing extra cards or healing Pokémon.

Provide the energy needed to use attacks and abilities.

Trainer Card Types

  • Item Cards: Can be played anytime to provide an effect.
  • Supporter Cards: Powerful effects but only one can be played per turn.
  • Stadium Cards: Stay in play and affect both players.

Energy Card Types

  • Basic Energy: Fire, Water, Grass, Electric, Psychic, Fighting, Dark, Metal, Fairy.
  • Special Energy: Provides multiple energy types or special effects.
