Welcome to the Pokémon Trading Card Game!

Introduction to Pokémon TCG

The Pokémon Trading Card Game (TCG) is a collectible card game where players build decks around their favorite Pokémon and battle against others.

Getting Started Guide

  • Get a **starter deck** or build your own.
  • Learn the basic rules and mechanics.
  • Play against friends or in tournaments.
  • Collect rare cards to enhance your deck!
