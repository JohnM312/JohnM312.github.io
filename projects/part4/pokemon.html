Getting Started Guide

If you're new to the Pokémon TCG, follow these steps to begin your journey:

  1. Get a 60-card deck with a mix of Pokémon, Energy, and Trainer cards.
  2. Learn the basic rules and how to play your cards by clicking the "Rules" link above!.
  3. Start battling with friends or enter tournaments to test your skills!
