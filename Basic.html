Cancel Culture's in the Modern Era

Cancel Culture Effect in the Negative

Cancel Culture is a prevalent issue in today's society. These cases are usually common towards influencers that are caught in controversies.. Most cases these people would lose lots of respect in people and end up with a negative perception from their fanbase. Sometimes Cancel Culture can be so extreme that it would affect them for the rest of their lives.

  • Ruins Public Image
  • Lose Respect
  • Lifetime Consequences

How Cancel Culture Effect in the Positive

However, there are instances where Cancel Culture can sometime benefit influencers. One good example would be Logan Paul, where he was canceled for making a video in Aokigahara forest making jokes and disrespecting their culture. After many backlashes from his fans, he took a hiatus and returned with a new mindset. Eventually his fans began to respect him on how much he matured after the incident.
